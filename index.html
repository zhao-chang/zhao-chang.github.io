I am an assistant/associate professor in NSS Lab, School of Computer Science and Technology, Xidian University. My research work focuses on privacy and security issues in large-scale data management.

Before joining Xidian University, I obtained my Ph.D. degree in Computing at University of Utah in 2021. My Ph.D. advisors are Prof. Feifei Li and Prof. Jeff M. Phillips.

I also obtained my B.S. degree in Computer Science and Technology at Peking University in 2013, and worked closely with Prof. Lei Zou and Prof. Jun Gao.
